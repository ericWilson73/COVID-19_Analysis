COVID-19 study

2022-11-29

Purpose

To identify notable trends in both new cases and deaths from COVID-19 in select countries. Possible biases in the data could prevent accurate reporting. One bias could be that certain authoritarian countries will not report their numbers accurately. But an even bigger issue could be that, as vaccines and treatments alleviate symptoms, citizens will stop self-reporting when they have COVID. This will render results less trustworthy.
